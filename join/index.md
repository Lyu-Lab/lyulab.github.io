# Positions

The Lyu lab is actively recruiting scientists at every career stage. We welcome applicants from **all** backgrounds. General inquiries can be sent to JK at [jlyu@rockefeller.edu](mailto:jlyu@rockefeller.edu).

## Postdoctoral Fellows

We are looking for motivated postdocs. To apply, email JK at [jlyu@rockefeller.edu](mailto:jlyu@rockefeller.edu) with a **one-page cover letter and CV**.

Ideal qualifications:

- A recent Ph.D. in computational chemistry, biology, physics, biophysics, or a related field
- A strong background in computational chemistry and HPC computing
- Expertise in molecular docking and protein structural modeling
- Strong programming and analytical skills

## Graduate Students

Students currently enrolled in one of our affiliated programs can email [jlyu@rockefeller.edu](mailto:jlyu@rockefeller.edu) to inquire about rotations:

- [David Rockefeller Graduate Program](https://www.rockefeller.edu/graduate/)
- [Tri-Institutional Ph.D. Program in Chemical Biology](https://chembio.triiprograms.org/)
- [Tri-Institutional Ph.D. Program in Computational Biology & Medicine](https://compbio.triiprograms.org/)

Prospective graduate students should apply directly to any of these programs.

## Research Assistants

Candidates with a bachelor's degree in chemistry, biology, computer science, engineering, physics, or a related field can apply through the Rockefeller careers portal for **"Research Assistant – Laboratory of Computational Molecular Discovery."**

## Undergraduates

Local undergraduates interested in computational projects are encouraged to email [jlyu@rockefeller.edu](mailto:jlyu@rockefeller.edu).
